Overview

Friends is an iconic American TV sitcom that aired from 1994 to 2004. Created by David Crane and Marta Kauffman, the show revolves around six friends – Ross, Rachel, Monica, Chandler, Joey, and Phoebe – navigating life, love, and careers in New York City. With humor, heart, and memorable moments, Friends has become one of the most beloved TV shows of all time.

Main Characters

Rachel Green

Rachel starts as a runaway bride who finds herself working at Central Perk. Over the series, she grows into a successful fashion executive. Played by Jennifer Aniston.

Ross Geller

Ross is a paleontologist and Monica’s older brother. Known for his love of dinosaurs and his on-again, off-again relationship with Rachel. Played by David Schwimmer.

Monica Geller

A chef known for her perfectionism and competitive nature. Monica is the glue that holds the group together. Played by Courteney Cox.

Chandler Bing

Chandler is known for his sarcastic humor and mysterious job in statistical analysis and data reconfiguration. Played by Matthew Perry.

Joey Tribbiani

Joey is a struggling actor and food lover with a heart of gold. Famous for his catchphrase, "How you doin'?" Played by Matt LeBlanc.

Phoebe Buffay

Phoebe is an eccentric, free-spirited musician known for her quirky songs like "Smelly Cat." Played by Lisa Kudrow.
